I am Thomas Kelly, the sole designer and developer of Recite. I created Recite for my Interaction Design (BA) Major Project at NCAD, Dublin.

Recite took me months of refining, testing with tons of users and gaining feedback from other designers.

Recite's prototypes were made usign HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and Figma. I am continuing to develop prototypes, and next will be a fully interactive mobile prototype made using Flutter, and industry standard mobile application development framework.

Feel free to contact me on LinkedIn or by email if you have any feedback or wish to support the project going forward.
